1. Introduction

Welcome to "Sporreer," a service provided by Shanghai Wabala Technology Co., Ltd. (hereinafter referred to as "we" or "Wabala Technology"). We attach great importance to the privacy and personal information protection of our users (hereinafter referred to as "you"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect the personal information you provide or generate through our internet platform, future apps, and related services.

2. The information we collected

When you use our platform or services, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Registration Information: When you register an account, the personal information you fill in, such as username, email address, mobile phone number (if registration or verification is required), password (encrypted storage), avatar, nickname, etc. (if you choose to provide this information).
  • Competition and sports data: Your performance and race records in competitive activities, competition-related metadata (such as time, location, track type, etc.), sensor data (e.g., if your platform has connected sports equipment: acceleration, angular velocity, etc.), and information about your behavior on the platform.
  • Device and usage information: Your hardware device model, operating system version, device unique identifier, application usage logs (such as startup time, page visits, error logs), IP address, network type, and language settings, etc.
  • Location Information: If you are participating in competitions that require location information, we may need to collect your phone's GPS coordinates, location history, and other information. With your consent, we may continuously collect your running trajectory and route data in the background.
  • Communication information: This includes information you provide when communicating with our customer service or technical support, and content you submit when participating in surveys, feedback, or activities.
  • Third-party service data collection: We may integrate third-party analytics tool SDKs, which may collect data related to your device and usage, such as the use of payment, advertising, or promotional features. These third-party services may be involved.

3. How do we use this information

We collect the above information from you primarily for the following purposes:

  • Services provided and maintained: for account management, competition registration, score recording, data statistics, etc.
  • Optimize user experience: Analyze how you use the app, determine which features are popular, and diagnose bugs/crashes.
  • Notifications and Communication: We will send you notifications related to competitions, platform feature updates, customer service responses, and more.
  • Security and Risk Control: Detecting abnormal behavior, preventing cheating or abuse, and ensuring platform fairness.
  • Marketing and Promotion: With the user's consent, this may be used for promotion, event invitations, etc.

4. Information sharing and disclosure

We will not sell your personal information to third parties. However, we may share or disclose your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may provide your information to third-party service providers we work with (such as data analytics, identity verification, cloud storage, email service providers, etc.) so that they can help us implement our service functions.
  • Legal Requirements: If laws, regulations, or government agencies require us to disclose your information, we will cooperate to the extent permitted by law.
  • Business Transfer: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or restructuring (such as an asset transfer), your information may be transferred accordingly. However, we will require the receiving party to continue to comply with this Privacy Policy.
  • Anonymous/Aggregated Data: We may use or disclose statistical data in an anonymous or aggregated form (where it cannot identify an individual) for analysis, research, or reporting purposes.

5. Information storage and security

We will store your personal information on secure servers and take reasonable technical and management protection measures to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration or destruction.

For sensitive data (such as sensor trajectories and location data), we will encrypt the storage or restrict access.

Your personal account information will be retained by default and will be deleted immediately upon account cancellation. Competition data such as GPS tracks will be retained for up to 6 months for data analysis.

In the event of a data breach, we will respond promptly and notify users in accordance with applicable laws.

6. Your Data Rights

As a user, you have the following rights (subject to applicable jurisdiction):

  • Access rights: You may request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Right to correction: If your information is incorrect or incomplete, you can request a correction.
  • Right of deletion: You may request that we delete your personal information in reasonable circumstances (e.g., after you close your account).
  • Restricted processing: You may request that we restrict the processing of certain data.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: You may withdraw your consent at any time if certain data processing is based on your consent (such as marketing emails).
  • Data portability: Subject to applicable laws, you may request that your data be exported in a portable format.

You can exercise your data rights through official emails or phone numbers, or through the feedback channels within the app.

7. Children's privacy

For sports competitions involving minors, we will clearly state the age restrictions during the registration process and obtain the consent of parents/guardians. We will not intentionally collect sensitive personal information of minors without obtaining proper consent.

8. Cookies and related technologies

We may use cookies/local storage/similar tracking technologies on the web or mobile to improve user experience, remember settings, and analyze usage.

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings or device settings, but this may affect the use of some functions.

9. International data transmission

If we transfer personal data to servers outside your country (e.g., if the cloud service provider is located in another country), we will ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to comply with applicable data protection laws.

10. Privacy Policy Update

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Each time a change is made, we will post the new effective date on the platform and notify users of significant changes via in-app notifications or email. Please check this page regularly for the latest updates.
